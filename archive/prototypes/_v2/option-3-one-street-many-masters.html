Presenter

About this concept

One Street, Many Masters — one evidence pack, four organisations, four opposing campaigns.

User & problem

A staffer at each of four organisations — the point is that it's the same tool. The audience, not the user, does the deciding: what does “grassroots” mean when campaigns are manufactured from a shared record?

Central demonstration idea

Facts frozen by code; framing swapped by a toggle. The wow is watching identical claims become opposite campaigns, live, with a drift checker proving no fact moved.

Main flow

Model-only opinion (uncited) → evidence collected → pack frozen (EP-041) → frame toggle: two live opposed syntheses → all four frames → approval gate → “any street, any master” view.

What's real

Livethe frame toggle and both re-syntheses — the wow is genuinely live (smallest live surface of the four options)
Cachedthe frozen evidence pack; frames 3–4 (Leicester sources verified open 11 Jul 2026)
Deterministiccitation binding to EP-041; the drift diff; approval state
Agenticper-frame strategy and drafting; drift judgement calls
Simulatedthe four organisations (fictional composites — naming real orgs would be the one genuinely irresponsible move)

Benefits

Cleanest single idea; cheapest reliable wow (one cached pack + two model calls); directly stages synthetic campaigning and evidentiary laundering.

Risks & tensions

No geographic scale — the factory point lands via multiplicity, not volume. Risk of reading as “AI is a spin machine”, an older point than “AI is an organising machine.” Fixable with the hybrid ending (scale frame 4 across wards).

Build complexity

Lowest of the four. Kit mirrors Base UI; production swaps to @base-ui-components/react.

In a 10-minute demo

Highest reliability, clearest idea. If models converge instead of diverging in rehearsal, the concept dies — that's the kill criterion.
