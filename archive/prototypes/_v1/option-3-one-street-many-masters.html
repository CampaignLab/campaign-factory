Honest assessment

Strengths: the cleanest single idea of the four — understandable in one sentence, visually symmetric, and the wow moment (live toggle, opposite campaigns) is also the cheapest to make reliable: one cached evidence pack, two live model calls. Lowest build risk. Directly stages the synthetic-campaigning and evidentiary-laundering panel themes. No real organisation is impersonated.
Weaknesses: no “scale” dimension — the factory reveal is four frames, not four hundred places, so the centralisation/volunteer-displacement themes land less hard. The audience may read it as “AI is a spin machine”, which is a narrower (and older) point than “AI is an organising machine.” Fictional orgs slightly soften the punch.
