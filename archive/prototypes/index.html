The Campaign Planner

The Leicester school street taken all the way: sense-making, an objective that passes the SMART check, a power map with stated/inferred badges, pressure analysis, a strategy, sequenced tactics, a supporter ladder, six resource packs, metrics, and an export gate. Nine specialist agents contribute; four of their disagreements are shown and resolved on the record. Built on the two campaign-workshop decks.

The Scout

A research department for one campaigner: postcode in, cited campaign pack out. The factory fans specialist read-only research agents across every school street in England — 412 packs overnight, one human inbox.

Prototype 2 · electoral · fear 5/5

The Ward Machine

Local knowledge without locals: head office feeds target wards into the hopper; electoral-analysis, research, drafting and review agents return doorstep-ready campaigns. 120 wards, zero volunteers consulted.

Prototype 3 · pluralism · fear 4/5

One Street, Many Masters

Manufactured pluralism: frame agents run in parallel against one frozen evidence pack, a drift-checker keeps every fact locked, and opposed campaigns emerge — for any street, for any organisation on the register.

Ends: any street × any masterBuild: lowest

The Consultation Hunter

Machine-speed participation: feed adapters pour every opening consultation into a queue, drafting agents respond to each in the cloud, and a human signs everything. 1,400 windows tracked; officers reading them: unchanged.
